1 Usage

For example ✆.

  • ➤

    The first item.

  • ➤

    The second item in the list.

  • ➤

    A final item.

  1. ➀

    The first item in the list.

  2. ➁

    The second item in the list.

  3. ➂

    The third item in the list.

  1. 1.
    1. ➀

      first

    2. ➁

      second

    3. ➂

      third

  2. 2.
    1. ❶

      first

    2. ❷

      second

    3. ❸

      third

References to list items work as expected: ➀ ‣ 1, ➁ ‣ 1, ➂ ‣ 1. As well as ➀ ‣ 1,➁ ‣ 1,➂ ‣ 1, and ❶ ‣ 2,❷ ‣ 2,❸ ‣ 2.
