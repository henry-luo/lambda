1 Usage

For example ✆.

  • The first item.

  • The second item in the list.

  • A final item.

  1. The first item in the list.

  2. The second item in the list.

  3. The third item in the list.

    1. first

    2. second

    3. third

    1. first

    2. second

    3. third

References to list items work as expected: ➀ ‣ 1, ➁ ‣ 1, ➂ ‣ 1. As well as ➀ ‣ 1,➁ ‣ 1,➂ ‣ 1, and ❶ ‣ 2,❷ ‣ 2,❸ ‣ 2.
