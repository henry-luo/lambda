Phase 3 — Controls + Polish

Video controls overlay, poster, preload, error handling, click-to-play

1. Controls overlay

Video with controls attribute — should show play/pause, seek bar, time, volume at bottom.

Expected: 40px dark bar at bottom with play/pause button, current time, seek bar, duration, volume icon + slider.

2. Controls + autoplay + loop

Autoplaying video with controls — bar should show pause icon and advancing time.

Expected: Video plays immediately. Pause icon visible. Time advances. Seek bar fills. Loops at end.

3. Click to play/pause (no controls attr)

No controls — click video body to toggle play/pause. Large centered play button when paused.

Expected: Large play button centered. Click → starts playing (button disappears). Click again → pauses (button reappears).

4. Poster image

Video with poster attribute — should show poster before playback.

Expected: Shows teal "Poster Image" graphic. Click → video plays, poster replaced by video frame + controls.

5. Preload="none"

Video with preload="none" — should not open/decode until play is triggered.

Expected: Shows idle state (play button). File not opened until user clicks to play.

6. Preload="none" + autoplay

When both set, autoplay should override preload="none".

Expected: Video plays immediately despite preload="none". Autoplay takes precedence.

7. Error state — missing file

Non-existent file — should show error overlay (dark bg + red X).

Expected: Dark rectangle with red X drawn. No crash.

8. Muted + controls

Muted autoplay with controls — volume slider should reflect muted state.

Expected: Video plays with no audio. Controls visible with volume at full (muted state not yet visually reflected).

9. Multiple videos side by side

Two videos in a row — each should have independent controls.

Expected: Left video autoplays (muted). Right shows play button. Click each independently.

10. Small video (100×60)

Very small video — controls overlay may be suppressed (min 120×60 threshold).

Expected: Controls not shown (too small). Video still plays. Click toggles play/pause.
