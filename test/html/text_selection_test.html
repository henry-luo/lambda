Text Selection & Caret Test

Test Instructions:

  • Click on any text below to position the caret
  • Click and drag to select text
  • Use Shift+Click to extend selection
  • Press Ctrl+A / Cmd+A to select all text
  • Press Ctrl+C / Cmd+C to copy selected text to clipboard
  • Use arrow keys to move the caret
  • Hold Shift while using arrow keys to extend selection

This is a simple paragraph of text that you can click on to position the text cursor (caret). Try clicking at different positions within the text.

You can also click and drag your mouse to select portions of text. The selected text should be highlighted with a blue background.

Once you have text selected, press Ctrl+C (or Cmd+C on macOS) to copy it to the clipboard. Then you can paste it in another application to verify the copy worked!
